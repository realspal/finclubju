The Journey Begins

Exploring the origins of FinClub, the team's mission and the idea behind the only financial markets oriented club in Jadavpur University.
Finance Blog Analysis

“It all starts with an idea. But you can never tell where an idea will end up. Because ideas spread, they change, grow, they connect us with the world.”

For us at FinClub JU, it started with a group of passionate investors and traders, committed to the idea of building a community of finance enthusiasts like ourselves. The idea spread, it changed and grew, and we formed the first and only financial markets oriented club in the university, the Jadavpur University Finance Club, also known as FinClub.

We are a student community of finance enthusiasts on a mission to promote financial literacy and provide a platform to facilitate discussion and knowledge sharing on everything finance. Our mission is to provide the perfect place to enhance financial knowledge and to promulgate financial literacy within the student community and beyond.

The driving force for our work is our faith in the ability of every individual to aspire higher, develop their dreams and make it their destiny. Our objectives include spreading general awareness of national as well as international news, inculcating students with the habit of staying informed and updated, creating a strong finance culture which benefits the entire student community and providing a platform for growth, a shared investment in knowledge which is destined to yield huge returns for all.

We have just started building this community of finance enthusiasts and passionate investors and traders. Within a short span of time we have expanded our reach to students in every department of the university. Our long agenda for the first year of the club includes a plethora of activities like workshops, seminars, speaker sessions and a variety of other events which will help in developing financial instincts among young minds as well as make it more engaging for our members and the entire student community.

With so much happening in the world of finance on a regular basis, staying well-versed with the news and updated on every significant incident has never been more crucial. Time moves at a rapid pace in today's world and comprehending the very factors which affect our daily lives often becomes an arduous task. The explanatory news articles, opinion pieces, analysis reports and educational publications on our finance blog and the newsletter aim to cut through the complicated jargon and break down complex financial concepts and news headlines into a simplified form.

At Jadavpur University Finance Club, we are dedicated to our vision of providing financial knowledge in a comprehensive and organized form so that every member joining our community with a desire to learn about financial markets leaves as a confident individual with a strong knowledge of the financial world.
