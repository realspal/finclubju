Membership & Mentorship

Any student or alumnus of Jadavpur University can join FinClub.

Membership of Jadavpur University Finance Club is open to all students pursuing higher education in any course under Jadavpur University. Alumni of the university are also invited to join as mentors.

You can join FinClub by :
  • Submitting the membership form given on our website.
  • Contacting your class FinClub Coordinator.
  • Applying for membership during the Annual Recruitment Season.

At Jadavpur University Finance Club, we are dedicated to our vision of providing financial knowledge in a comprehensive and organized form so that every member joining our community with a desire to learn about financial markets leaves as a confident individual with a strong knowledge of the financial world. Our mission is to provide the perfect place to enhance financial knowledge and to promulgate financial literacy within the student community and beyond.

The Jadavpur University Finance Club is a student community of finance enthusiasts on a mission to promote financial literacy and provide a platform to facilitate discussion and knowledge sharing on everything finance. Our long agenda for the year includes a plethora of activities like workshops, seminars, speaker sessions and a variety of other events that help in developing financial instincts among young minds as well as make it more engaging for our members and the student community.
