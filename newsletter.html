About our Newsletter

With so much happening in the world of finance on a regular basis, staying well-versed with the news and updated on every significant incident has never been more crucial. Time moves at a rapid pace in today's world and comprehending the very factors which affect our daily lives often becomes an arduous task.

Keeping in mind the needs and expectations of our readers, we are launching our own specially curated newsletter to cut through the complicated jargon and break down complex financial concepts and news headlines into a simplified form.

We aim to provide a comprehensive solution packed with insightful data to help our readers stay updated on trending issues and major happenings in the financial world through our exclusive coverage of current affairs, trading and investment analysis reports and other informative opinion pieces.

Business spending and investments are realistic indicators of financial markets and the entire economy. What happens in the stock market has a very strong impact on our daily lives and everything around us, which makes financial news an indispensable guide for taking major financial decisions in life.

At Jadavpur University Finance Club (FinClub JU), we are on a mission to promote financial literacy, spread general awareness of national as well as international news and inculcate the habit of staying informed and updated.
