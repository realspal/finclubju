About FinClub, J.U.

The Jadavpur University Finance Club is a student community of finance enthusiasts on a mission to promote financial literacy and provide a platform to facilitate discussion and knowledge sharing on everything finance.

Read our story Meet the team

Our Mission and Objectives

Founded in 2021 by a group of finance enthusiasts, the Jadavpur University Finance Club is the only financial markets oriented club in the university. Our mission is to provide the perfect place to enhance financial knowledge and to promulgate financial literacy within the student community and beyond.

The driving force for our work is our faith in the ability of every individual to aspire higher, develop their dreams and make it their destiny. Our objectives include creating a strong finance culture which benefits the entire student community and providing a platform for growth, a shared investment in knowledge which is destined to yield huge returns for all.

Our Work and Activities

We have just started building a community of finance enthusiasts and passionate investors and traders. Within a short span of time we have expanded our reach to students in every department of the university. Our long agenda for the year includes a plethora of activities like workshops, seminars, speaker sessions and a variety of other events that help in developing financial instincts among young minds as well as make it more engaging for our members and the student community.

At Jadavpur University Finance Club, we are dedicated to our vision of providing financial knowledge in a comprehensive and organized form so that every member joining our community with a desire to learn about financial markets leaves as a confident individual with a strong knowledge of the financial world.
